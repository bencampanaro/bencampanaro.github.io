Media Theory, History, & Criticism

CLU - Fall 2002

Here you can view some of the final projects produced in my Media Theory, History, & Criticism course, headed by Professor Daniel Restuccio.
The assignment was to develop a concept for an original holiday, with all the various aspects such as songs, traditions, colors, media, food, etc. fleshed out.
For our final, we had to pitch our concepts to a group of visitors.
